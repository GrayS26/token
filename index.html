HOW TO BUY?

STEP 1

Set Up a Wallet

Ensure you have a compatible Ethereum wallet that supports ERC-20 tokens. Popular options include MetaMask, Trust Wallet, or Coinbase Wallet. If you don't have a wallet, create one and securely store your private key.

STEP 2

Add Ethereum to Your Wallet

Fund your wallet with enough Ethereum (ETH) to cover the cost of the ERC-20 tokens you intend to purchase and transaction fees.

STEP 3

Navigate to Uniswap

Visit the Uniswap website (https://app.uniswap.org/) and connect your wallet by clicking the "Connect to a Wallet" button. Choose your wallet provider and follow the prompts to connect.

STEP 4

Select Token Pair

Once connected, select the token pair you wish to trade. Click on "Select a token" and enter the token's contract address or choose from the list of available tokens.

STEP 5

Enter the Amount

Specify the amount of the ERC-20 token you want to buy. Uniswap will automatically calculate the equivalent amount in ETH.

STEP 6

Adjust Slippage Tolerance (Optional)

If you encounter issues with the transaction, you may need to adjust the slippage tolerance. Click on the gear icon and set an appropriate slippage percentage (usually 1-2%).

STEP 7

Confirm Transaction

Review the transaction details, including the estimated gas fees. Click "Swap" and confirm the transaction in your wallet. Ensure you have enough ETH to cover both the token purchase and gas fees.

STEP 8

Confirm in Wallet

Approve the transaction in your wallet. This step may require an additional confirmation, depending on your wallet provider.

STEP 9

Wait for Confirmation

Wait for the transaction to be confirmed on the Ethereum blockchain. This may take a few minutes, depending on network congestion.

STEP 10

Check your Wallet

Once the transaction is confirmed, check your wallet to confirm the presence of the newly acquired ERC-20 tokens.

Congratulations! You have successfully purchased ERC-20 tokens on Uniswap. Keep in mind that cryptocurrency transactions involve risks, and it's essential to double-check all details before confirming any transactions.
